📊 HOW SCORING WORKS — ROUND-END FLOW

Run all the questions in a round (read prompts, play clips, teams shout/write). At the end of the round, hit the 📊 SCORE ROUND button (or press S) — a modal pops with all teams + a number input each. Type each team's points, hit ✓ APPLY & CONTINUE ▶. Scores apply, game advances to next round.

Round caps: R1=20 · R2=16 · R3=15 · R4=8 · R5=16 · Final=20 · TOTAL=95 pts

Bottom scoreboard +/- buttons are still there for mid-round adjustments. Leader auto-gets 👑.
